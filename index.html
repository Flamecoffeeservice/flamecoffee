About Us

At FLAME Coffee Service, we specialize in fast, professional maintenance and repair of premium coffee equipment. Our team is certified by La Marzocco and experienced with multiple brands.

We want every guest to enjoy coffee brewed on properly serviced and calibrated machines — to consciously taste the difference and get the most pleasure from every cup.

Our goal is not only repair and maintenance, but also fostering a culture of understanding how the condition of equipment impacts coffee quality and the overall business.

What sets us apart

• Deep understanding of the coffee process
We know not only how the equipment works, but also how its condition affects the taste. That’s why our service focuses not only on functionality, but also on the result in the cup.

• Modern approach to service
We work cleanly, carefully, and professionally. We have standards, unified processes, and respect for the client’s establishment.

• Flexible service formats
We offer subscriptions, equipment rental with service, one-time call-outs, and on-demand support. The client can choose the format that best suits their business.

• Transparent terms and fair pricing
Every service is clearly described in advance. Costs are agreed upon before any work begins. No unexpected charges or unclear services.

• Expertise in equipment development and manufacturing
We have hands-on experience in creating coffee machines, which allows us to find unconventional solutions and understand the equipment on a deeper level.

• Simple and clear communication
We explain the issue, the ways to resolve it, and how it affects the equipment and coffee quality — without technical jargon unless necessary.

Trusted by cafes, roasteries, and hospitality businesses across Portugal.
